# 6students | Team Portfolio Project

**⚠️ Project Disclaimer:** The works listed in the "Projects" section are samples created specifically for academic purposes and to demonstrate our technical capabilities.

### 🚀 Our Project
We built this website as a collaborative team portfolio to showcase our collective growth as IT students. Our goal was to create a modern, high-performance landing page that highlights our combined skills in development, design, and system optimization. 

---

### 🛠️ Our Tech Stack
We selected these tools to ensure our site is fast, responsive, and visually engaging:
* **Tailwind CSS**: Used for our rapid styling and ensuring the layout stays consistent across all devices.
* **JavaScript (ES6+)**: Powers our site logic, including menu toggling and the FAQ accordion.
* **AOS (Animate On Scroll)**: Provides the smooth entrance animations you see as you explore our sections.
* **Particles.js**: Created our interactive starfield background to match our modern aesthetic.

---

### 📂 Our Website Sections
* **Hero**: We designed this to make a strong first impression, featuring a bold headline that defines our team's purpose.
* **About**: We organized this to highlight our professional services and our technical stack (C++, Java, JS) using glowing glassmorphism cards.
* **Work**: This section is all about showing our coding samples with interactive hover effects and specific language tags.
* **Team**: We built a responsive grid to introduce all six of us, featuring hover animations and direct links to our profiles.
* **FAQ**: We implemented a functional expansion panel to answer common questions while keeping our layout clean and tidy.
* **Contact**: We integrated a direct form using Formspree so visitors can reach our team easily without needing a complex backend.

---

### 🔧 How to View Our Work
1. Clone this repository or download the project ZIP.
2. Open the project folder on your computer.
3. Launch `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave).
